<Section icon={Trophy} title="Maximizing Exotic Seed Potential with Mutations">
Acquiring an **Exotic Seed** is a huge step, but its true power can be unleashed through mutations. The mutation system in Build A Ring Farm can significantly multiply the income generated by your plants, turning an already valuable Exotic crop into an absolute goldmine.

Mutations are random events that occur during specific in-game events or can be applied using special sprays bought from the Gear Shop. Each mutation adds a unique visual effect and, more importantly, a money multiplier to the plant.

| Mutation     | Multiplier | Appearance         | How to Get                                         | Cost (Spray)       |
| :----------- | :--------- | :----------------- | :------------------------------------------------- | :----------------- |
| Wet          | **1.5x**   | Water-like         | 8% chance during Rain Event                        | $10M               |
| Frozen       | **1.75x**  | Frozen             | 4% chance during Blizzard Event                    | $750M              |
| Void         | **2.25x**  | Black              | 3% chance during Black Hole Event                  | $1B                |
| Radioactive  | **3x**     | Green neon         | 2% chance during Nuclear Event                     | $10B               |
| Rainbow      | **5x**     | Dynamic colors     | 1% chance during Galaxy Event                      | $1T                |

<Callout type="success" title="The Power of Rainbow Mutation">
The **Rainbow** mutation is currently the best in Build A Ring Farm, offering a staggering **5x multiplier**. While it's the hardest to get (1% chance during the Galaxy Event or a $1T spray), applying it to an Exotic Seed can result in astronomical profits, making it the ultimate goal for any serious farmer.
</Callout>

**Important Mutation Facts:**
- **Offline Mutations:** Mutations will not apply to your plants if you're offline. You must be actively in the game during events to have a chance of your plants mutating.
- **Multiple Mutations:** A plant can only have one mutation at a time. If a plant already has a mutation, it won't catch another unless you remove the current one using an Acid Spray ($1M from the Gear Shop).

</Section>

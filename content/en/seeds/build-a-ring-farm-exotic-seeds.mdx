## Planning Your Ring Farm Layout for Maximum Efficiency

The key to a successful exotic seed operation is meticulous planning. A well-designed ring farm maximizes space, automates processes, and sets the stage for exponential growth.

*   **Central Collection Point:** Design your farm with a central collection area. This is where all your harvested crops will converge, ideally handled by automated collectors.
*   **Concentric Rings:** Build your planting plots in concentric rings around this central point. This circular design is efficient for automated sprinklers and harvesters.
*   **Upgrade Placement:** Strategically place your upgrade stations (e.g., speed upgrades, yield boosters) to cover the maximum number of plots within their radius.
*   **Pathways and Access:** While automation is key, ensure you have accessible pathways for manual intervention if needed, especially during initial setup or for placing new exotic seeds.
*   **Expansion Zones:** Always plan for future expansion. Your initial ring farm should be scalable, allowing you to add more rings as you acquire more land and resources.

Remember, the goal is to reduce manual input and increase yield per minute. Every square counts when you're aiming for quadrillions.

### Event-Based Mutations vs. Sprays

While sprays offer a guaranteed method to apply mutations, it's worth noting that mutations can also occur during specific in-game events. These events provide a chance for your plants to spontaneously mutate.

| Mutation | Event Chance |
| :------- | :----------- |
| **Wet** | 8% chance during Rain Event |
| **Frozen** | 4% chance during Blizzard Event |
| **Void** | 3% chance during Black Hole Event |
| **Radioactive** | 2% chance during Nuclear Event |
| **Rainbow** | 1% chance during Galaxy Event |

While event-based mutations are free, their low probability makes sprays a more reliable and controllable option for players aiming for consistent income optimization. Sprays allow you to target specific plants and mutations without waiting for rare in-game occurrences.

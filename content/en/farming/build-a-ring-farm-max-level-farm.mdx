<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Unlocking Advanced Mechanics: Mutations and Secrets">
Mutations are game-changers in Build A Ring Farm, offering massive multipliers to your plant's income. These can be obtained through random events or by purchasing specific sprays from the Gear Shop. Focusing on acquiring and applying the best mutations is paramount for a max level farm.

<Callout type="info" title="Mutation Mechanics">
Mutations will only apply if you are actively in the game during an event. They will not apply if you are offline. A plant can only have one mutation at a time.
</Callout>

  <Step num="1" title="Initial Placement & Layout">

  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Basic Automation">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Upgrade Crafting Stations">
    Once basic automation is in place, upgrade your crafting stations to produce higher-tier rings. This significantly boosts your per-ring profit. Balance this with the cost of new recipes and materials.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Expand Your Farm Strategically">
    As you accumulate wealth, expand your farm by purchasing more land or plots. Plan your expansion to maintain efficiency, perhaps creating dedicated zones for different ring types or production stages.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Acquire Exotic Seeds">
    Exotic seeds are crucial for late-game wealth. Participate in events, complete special quests, or trade with other players to get your hands on these valuable resources. Rings from exotic seeds sell for massive amounts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Common Pitfall">

</Callout>

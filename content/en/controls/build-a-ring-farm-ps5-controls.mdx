### Controller Sensitivity and Vibration

Adjusting your controller sensitivity is a personal preference, but finding the right balance can make a big difference in how smoothly you interact with the game. A moderate sensitivity setting is often recommended for precision in placing items and navigating.

| Setting          | Recommended Value | Impact on Gameplay                                   |
| :--------------- | :---------------- | :--------------------------------------------------- |
| **Sensitivity**  | Mid-range         | Affects cursor speed and camera movement.            |
| **Vibration**    | Off               | Eliminates controller rumble, reduces distraction.   |
| **Reduce Motion**| On (if lagging)   | Minimizes visual effects to improve frame rate.      |
| **Camera Invert**| Off (default)     | Standard camera controls; invert for opposite movement.|

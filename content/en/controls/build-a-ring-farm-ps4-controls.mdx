### Steps to Plant and Sell Your First Crop

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Seeds">
    Approach a seed vendor or use a seed rolling station. Press **Square** to purchase or roll for seeds. Ensure you have enough in-game currency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select a Plot">
    Move your character to an empty farm plot. The game will usually highlight available plots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Plant the Seed">
    With a seed in your inventory, press **Square** to plant it on the selected plot. Watch for the growth timer to begin.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Harvest Crops">
    Once the crop is fully grown, approach it and press **Square** to harvest. The harvested crop will be added to your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Sell for Profit">
    Go to the selling point on your farm. With the harvested crops in your inventory, press **Square** to sell them and earn money.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Target} title="Optimizing Your Build a Ring Farm Settings for PS4/PS5">
Beyond just controls, optimizing your game settings can significantly enhance your Build a Ring Farm experience. Adjusting graphics, turning off vibration, and fine-tuning sensitivity can lead to smoother gameplay and better performance, especially on PS4 or older PS5 models.

<Callout type="warning" title="Performance Alert">
If you experience significant lag, immediately access the settings via the **Options button** and set your graphics mode to **Manual** with low settings. This can drastically improve performance.
</Callout>

### Recommended Settings Adjustments

| Setting Category     | Option           | Recommended Value/Action                                      | Impact on Gameplay                                     |
|----------------------|------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------|
| **Graphics Mode**    | Manual           | Low (if lagging), High (if stable)                            | Directly affects visual quality and frame rate.        |
| **Vibration**        | Off              | Turn off                                                      | Reduces controller distraction, saves battery.         |
| **Reduce Motion**    | On               | Turn on if experiencing lag                                   | Helps stabilize frame rate and reduce visual stutter.  |
| **Camera Invert**    | Off              | Keep off (unless you prefer inverted camera controls)         | Changes vertical camera movement direction.            |
| **Sensitivity**      | Mid-range        | Adjust to personal preference (not too high or low)           | Affects cursor and camera movement speed.              |
| **Volume**           | Adjustable       | Set to comfortable level                                      | Controls in-game music and sound effects.              |
| **Chat Translation** | Language Select  | Choose your preferred language                                | Translates in-game chat messages.                      |

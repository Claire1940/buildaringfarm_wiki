<Section icon={Trophy} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Adjusting Settings">
Follow these simple steps to quickly configure your controller settings in Build A Ring Farm for optimal performance on your PS4 or PS5.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Launch Build A Ring Farm">
    Start the Build A Ring Farm experience on Roblox from your PS4 or PS5 console. Ensure the game is fully loaded to access all features.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Open Settings Menu">
    Once in the game, press the **touchpad** on your PlayStation controller. This will bring up a quick menu. From there, press the **Options** button to open the main settings interface.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Navigate to Display & Control Settings">
    In the settings menu, look for the 'Settings' icon, usually located in the top-left corner. Click on it to reveal a list of adjustable options, including graphics, sound, and control preferences.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Configure Graphics and Performance">
    - **Graphics Mode**: Change this to 'Manual' and slide the quality bar to 'Low' if you're experiencing frame rate drops or lag.
    - **Reduce Motion**: Toggle this option 'On' to further smooth out visual performance, especially on older systems.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Adjust Control and Comfort Settings">
    - **Vibration**: Locate the 'Vibration' option and turn it 'Off' for a more focused gameplay experience.
    - **Sensitivity**: Adjust the 'Sensitivity' slider to your preferred level. A setting around the middle is generally a good starting point for balanced control in Build A Ring Farm.
    - **Camera Invert**: Ensure 'Camera Invert' is 'Off' unless you specifically prefer inverted camera controls.
  </Step>
</Steps>

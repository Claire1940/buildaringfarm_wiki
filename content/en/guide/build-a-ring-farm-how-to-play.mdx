The core gameplay loop revolves around planting seeds, waiting for them to grow, harvesting the mature crops, and then selling them for in-game currency. This money is then reinvested into better seeds, upgrades, and sprays to further boost your income.

### Core Gameplay Loop

| Action       | Description                                           | Primary Goal                                  |
| :----------- | :---------------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------- |
| **Planting** | Place seeds on your farm plots using the `Square` button (PlayStation). | Grow crops for income.                        |
| **Harvesting** | Pick up mature crops.                                 | Prepare for selling.                          |
| **Selling**  | Exchange harvested crops for money.                   | Earn currency for upgrades.                   |
| **Upgrading**| Enhance existing plots or buy new items with `R2` or `Triangle`. | Increase yield and efficiency.                |
| **Removing** | Clear unwanted crops or plots with `L2` or `Triangle`. | Optimize farm layout.                         |

<Callout type="tip" title="Controller Tip">
On PlayStation, use the touchpad to select on-screen elements, the D-pad for navigation, and `Square` for planting seeds. `R2` is typically used for purchasing and `Triangle` for upgrading or removing crops.
</Callout>

### Understanding Mutations and Their Impact

| Mutation       | Multiplier & Appearance                               | How to Get                                    | Cost of Spray (Gear Shop) |
| :------------- | :---------------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------- | :------------------------ |
| **Wet**        | **1.5x** income, water-like appearance.               | 8% chance during Rain Event.                  | $10M                      |

<Callout type="warning" title="Mutation Limitations">
Mutations will not apply if you are offline. You must be in-game during events to have a chance at your plants mutating. Also, a plant can only have one mutation at a time.
</Callout>

To effectively get mutations, you can either rely on rare in-game events or purchase specific sprays from the Gear Shop. While event-based mutations are free, their occurrence is unpredictable and the chances are low. Investing in mutation sprays offers a more reliable, albeit costly, method to secure those valuable income multipliers.

<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding the Core Gameplay Loop">

Build a Ring Farm is a sophisticated Roblox farming simulator that integrates idle progression with gacha-style mechanics. Unlike traditional farming games where manual labor is the primary focus, this title emphasizes automation and strategic resource management. Your journey begins with a single ring-shaped plot, a basic sawmill, and a rotating sprinkler.

The fundamental loop consists of four primary actions: **Planting**, **Upgrading**, **Selling**, and **Repeating**. As you harvest crops using your automated sawmill, you generate cash that must be reinvested into your infrastructure. The game stands out by introducing a seed-rolling mechanic, where every pull of the lever grants a random seed rarity. This unpredictability dictates your growth trajectory and requires constant adaptation of your layout.

| Mechanic | Function | Importance |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Sawmill** | Automatically harvests mature crops in its path | **High** (Drives Income) |
| **Sprinkler** | Waters crops to ensure growth and mutation | **High** (Drives Speed) |
| **Seed Lever** | Grants random seeds based on Luck stats | **Critical** (Drives Tier) |
| **Seed Crusher** | Exchanges unwanted seeds for special rewards | **Medium** (Drives Efficiency) |

<Callout type="tip" title="Editor's Insight">
Always remember that your cash does not grow automatically in your wallet. You must actively collect and sell your harvest to realize your profits. In the early game, it is easy to get distracted by rolling seeds and forget to click the 'Sell' button, which halts your progression.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Priority Upgrades for Early Success">

In the first few hours of gameplay, your funds will be limited. Spreading your cash across every available upgrade is a recipe for stagnation. To grow fast, you must prioritize the mechanics that directly impact your "income per minute" (IPM).

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Saw Yield Optimization">
    Prioritize **Saw Yield** above all else. This stat determines how much crop your sawmill collects during each pass. If your yield is low, you are leaving money on the ground. Reach at least Level 8 before moving to other upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Sprinkler Power and Range">
    Your crops will not grow without water. **Sprinkler Power** increases growth speed, while **Range** ensures that as you expand to outer rings, every plot is covered. An unwatered plot is a wasted plot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Seed Luck Investment">
    Once your infrastructure is stable, invest in **Seed Luck**. This improves the RNG of the lever pulls, allowing you to bypass the grind of lower-tier seeds and jump straight into high-value crops like Melons or Pumpkins.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Upgrade | Primary Benefit | Recommended Early Level |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Saw Yield** | More cash per harvest | Level 10+ |
| **Sprinkler Power** | Faster growth cycles | Level 7 |
| **Saw Range** | Covers outer ring plots | Level 5 |
| **Seed Luck** | Higher rarity seed pulls | Level 10 |

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Strategy">
If you notice your sprinkler isn't reaching the outermost plots of your current ring, stop all other spending and focus exclusively on **Sprinkler Range**. Idle crops generate zero revenue, making those plots a liability until they are watered.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="The Mutation System: Multipliers Explained">

Mutations are the "secret sauce" of Build a Ring Farm. They apply visual effects and, more importantly, massive income multipliers to your crops. Understanding how to trigger and maintain these mutations is what separates average players from the top earners. Mutations occur randomly during specific weather events or can be forced using expensive sprays from the Gear Shop.

| Mutation | Multiplier | Appearance | Acquisition Method |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Wet** | **1.5x** | Water-like | Rain Event (8%) or Wet Spray |
| **Frozen** | **1.75x** | Frozen/Icy | Blizzard Event (4%) or Frozen Spray |
| **Void** | **2.25x** | Black/Dark | Black Hole Event (3%) or Void Spray |
| **Radioactive** | **3x** | Green Neon | Nuclear Event (2%) or Radioactive Spray |
| **Rainbow** | **5x** | Dynamic Colors | Galaxy Event (1%) or Rainbow Spray |

<Callout type="info" title="Mutation Rules">
- **No Stacking**: A plant can only hold one mutation at a time.
- **Persistence**: Mutations stay until the plant is replaced or cleared with Acid Spray.
- **Active Only**: Mutations will not apply while you are offline. You must be in-game during the event.
</Callout>

If you have a high-value crop that catches a lower-tier mutation like "Wet," you may eventually want to use **Acid Spray** ($1M) to clear it and try for a "Rainbow" mutation during a Galaxy event. However, for beginners, any mutation is a good mutation.

</Section>

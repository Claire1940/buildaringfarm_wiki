<Section icon={Map} title="How to Acquire and Manage Mutations">

Acquiring mutations can be done through two primary methods: waiting for natural events or purchasing Sprays from the Gear Shop. While events are free, they are RNG-dependent and require you to be online. Sprays provide a guaranteed path to success but come with a heavy price tag.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor Environmental Events">
    Stay in the server and watch for weather changes. Each event (like the Blizzard or Nuclear Event) provides a small percentage chance for your plants to mutate naturally. Note that you must be online for these to trigger.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear Low-Tier Mutations">
    Before trying for a high-tier mutation like Radioactive or Rainbow, use the **Acid Spray** ($1M) to clear any existing 1.5x or 1.75x multipliers from your plants.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply High-Tier Sprays">
    Once your plants are cleared, visit the Gear Shop. If you have the capital, purchase the Rainbow Spray for $1T to bypass the 1% event RNG.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Verify Multipliers">
    Check the unit income stats to ensure the multiplier has been applied correctly. The visual VFX should be immediately apparent.
  </Step>
</Steps>

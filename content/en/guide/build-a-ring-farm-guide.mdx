<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimizing Performance and Controls">

Whether you are playing on a PC or a console like the PS5, performance optimization is vital. As your farm grows and more particles from sprinklers and mutations appear, the game can become laggy. This lag directly impacts the timing of your saws and the efficiency of your growth cycles. Adjusting your settings early ensures that your farm runs at peak performance even when generating trillions of coins.

**Recommended Settings for Stability:**

| Setting Name | Recommended Value | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Graphics Mode** | Manual (Low) | Reduces lag during massive harvests |
| **Vibration** | Off | Prevents controller wear and distraction |
| **Reduce Motion** | On | Improves FPS in dense farm areas |
| **Camera Sensitivity** | 50% (Middle) | Balanced control for precise planting |
| **Chat Translation** | Preferred Language | Better communication with other players |

For console players, the touchpad and options menu are your primary hubs. You can toggle music to focus on the satisfying sound of harvesting or access the settings menu to lower the volume if the saws become too loud. Reducing motion is perhaps the most critical setting for players reaching the "billions" stage, as the sheer volume of visual data can crash lower-end devices.

<Callout type="warning" title="Lag Warning">
If you notice your crops are fully grown but the saw isn't picking them up immediately, your graphics settings are likely too high. Switch to manual mode and drop the slider to 1 or 2 bars.
</Callout>

</Section>

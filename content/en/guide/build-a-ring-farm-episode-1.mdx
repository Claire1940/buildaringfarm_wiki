### Maximizing Offline Earnings

While active play is key for events and mutations, **Build A Ring Farm** also offers offline earnings. This means your farm continues to generate cash even when you're not in the game.

- **Offline Earning Rules**: Your farm continues to produce income based on its current state and upgrades.
- **Maximize Idle Profit**: The more developed your farm is when you go offline, the more it will earn. Ensure your plots are full and machinery is upgraded before logging off.
- **Returning Player Tips**: When you return, collect your offline earnings immediately. Use this cash to reinvest, sell any ready crops, and then check for new events or available upgrades.

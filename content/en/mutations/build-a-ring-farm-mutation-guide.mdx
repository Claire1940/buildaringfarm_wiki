<Section icon={Map} title="How to Get Mutations: Events vs. Sprays">

There are two primary ways to apply mutations to your plants. The first is passive, relying on RNG during specific World Events. The second is active, involving the purchase of expensive Sprays from the Gear Shop. For most players, the **build a ring farm mutation guide** suggests using events early on and switching to Sprays once your income is stable.

### World Events
World Events occur randomly while you are online. You must be actively in the game for these events to trigger a mutation chance on your crops. If an event like a "Galaxy Event" starts, every plant on your farm has a 1% chance to transform into a Rainbow variant automatically.

### Gear Shop Sprays
If you don't want to wait for RNG, you can buy Sprays. These are guaranteed to apply the mutation but come with a heavy price tag.

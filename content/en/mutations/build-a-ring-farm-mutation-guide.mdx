## The Foundation: Early Game Growth and Essential Upgrades

Before you can chase those coveted mutations, a solid foundation is paramount. In the early stages of *Build a Ring Farm*, your primary focus should be on consistent income generation and unlocking crucial upgrades. Don't aim for instant riches; instead, strive for every plot to be constantly active.

Initially, prioritize fast-growing crops such as carrots, pumpkins, beetroot, and wheat. While these won't net you massive profits, their rapid growth ensures a steady stream of income, allowing you to expand and upgrade. A common pitfall for new players is neglecting sprinkler upgrades. Both **Sprinkler Power** and **Sprinkler Range** are, arguably, the most vital early-game investments. They directly influence crop growth speed, creating a continuous farming loop where crops automatically regrow and are harvested.

Alongside sprinklers, prioritize **Saw Range** and **Seed Luck**. Saw Range enhances harvesting efficiency, ensuring more crops are collected automatically. Seed Luck, on the other hand, is a game-changer. It significantly increases your chances of acquiring more valuable seeds later on, which is absolutely critical for long-term progression and the eventual quest for mutations.

## The Crop Evolution: From Common to Exotic

As your Seed Luck improves, you'll begin to unlock a wider array of more valuable crops. Replacing outdated plants with stronger seeds is a continuous process you should embrace. Even a single high-rarity crop can often outperform several weaker ones combined.

During your progression, keep an eye out for these powerful crops:

*   **Melon & Watermelon:** Good mid-game earners.
*   **Peach & Mushroom:** Provide a substantial boost to income.
*   **Glow Shrub:** A valuable step towards higher tiers.
*   **Golden Apple & Kiwi:** These are significant milestones, offering impressive returns.
*   **Cherry Blossom:** Another high-value crop that pushes your income further.

In the late game, your focus should shift entirely towards **Secret, Divine, and Exotic crops**. At this point, most players completely abandon lower-rarity plants. These high-tier crops scale much more effectively over time, forming the backbone of any truly infinite farm setup.

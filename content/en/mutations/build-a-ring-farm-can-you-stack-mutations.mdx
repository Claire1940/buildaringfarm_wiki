<Section icon={Shield} title="Can You Stack Mutations in Build a Ring Farm?">
A common question among players is whether mutations can be stacked on a single plant to achieve even higher income multipliers. The answer is straightforward: **No, plants cannot get multiple mutations in Build a Ring Farm.**

If a plant acquires any mutation during an event or through a spray, it will not acquire another one unless you first remove the current mutation. This means each plant is limited to one active mutation at any given time. While you can't stack them on a single plant, you can, of course, have multiple plants on your farm, each with its own unique mutation.

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Limitation">
Attempting to apply a new mutation to a plant that already has one will not result in stacking. The existing mutation must be removed first if you wish to apply a different one.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Acquiring and Managing Mutations">
Mutations can be acquired through two primary methods: natural occurrence during in-game events or by purchasing and using specific sprays from the Gear Shop.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor In-Game Events">
    Keep an eye on the in-game event timer. Each mutation is tied to a specific event (e.g., Rain for Wet, Galaxy for Rainbow) that offers a small chance for your plants to mutate naturally. You must be online and in the game for mutations to apply during these events.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Purchase Mutation Sprays">
    Visit the Gear Shop and purchase the corresponding spray for the mutation you desire. Sprays offer a guaranteed way to apply a mutation, though they can be very expensive, especially for higher-tier mutations.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply the Spray to Your Plant">
    Once you have the spray, select it from your inventory and use it on the plant you wish to mutate. Remember, if the plant already has a mutation, you'll need to remove it first.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Remove Unwanted Mutations (Optional)">
    If you want to change a plant's mutation or remove it entirely, purchase an **Acid Spray** from the Gear Shop for $1M. Using this spray will revert the plant to its unmutated state, allowing you to apply a new one.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Offline Play and Mutations">
Mutations will not apply to your plants if you are offline during an event. You need to be actively playing the game to benefit from the chance of natural mutations.
</Callout>

</Section>

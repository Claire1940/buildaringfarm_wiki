<Section icon={Target} title="Strategies for Acquiring Mutations">
Obtaining mutations requires a combination of patience, strategic play, and financial investment. Both in-game events and purchasing sprays from the Gear Shop are viable methods.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor In-Game Events">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Invest in Sprays">
    The Gear Shop offers sprays for each mutation. While expensive, especially for higher-tier mutations, these sprays guarantee a mutation application to your plants. This is a reliable method if you have the funds. For example, a Rainbow Spray costs $1 Trillion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prioritize High-Rarity Crops">
    Once you have a mutation spray or an event is active, apply it to your most valuable crops. Combining a 5x Rainbow mutation with a high-rarity crop like Golden Apple or Kiwi will yield significantly more profit than applying it to a basic crop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Strategic Farm Layout">
    Ensure your farm is optimized with strong sprinklers and saw range. This continuous farming loop maximizes the benefits of mutated crops by ensuring they grow and are harvested automatically and efficiently.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Upgrade Category | Priority | Impact on Mutation Efficiency                                                                |

| **Sprinkler Power** | High     | Directly controls crop growth speed, ensuring continuous harvest of mutated plants.          |
